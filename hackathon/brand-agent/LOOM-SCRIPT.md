# Loom script — AITX Brand Agent (AITX x NVIDIA Claw Hackathon)

**Format:** film loose (~10-15 min, many takes), cut to **~2 min, high energy,
green chair, your thing.** Latency doesn't matter — trim every "Nemotron
thinking…" wait in the edit. Everything runs on **NVIDIA Nemotron via NIM**.

**The one-liner:** *"An agent that learns your brand's rules and won't break
them. Powered by NVIDIA Nemotron. And it's the engine for a whole platform."*

**Three prizes at once:**
- Best Use of Nemotron (Nemotron is the brain; say why)
- Recursive Intelligence track (it learns from its mistakes)
- Most Commercializable / Antler (the platform + the church wedge)

---

## Pre-roll setup (before recording)

- Live app, full screen: **https://aitx-brand-os.vercel.app/agent**
  - Do one warm-up run first so the model is hot.
  - Browser zoom ~110-125%.
- The two example chips on `/agent` are pre-loaded and verified. Clicking the
  **first chip** (the hype marketing post) reliably makes the blind agent slip
  into hype and exclamation points; the knowledge base returns a warm, clean
  version. That's your hero click.
- Second tab: **/platform** (the pitch). Third tab: **/thanks** (thank a sponsor).

**Verified hero prompt (already the first chip):**
`Write a bold, exciting, high-energy marketing hype post to promote AITX's next big event. Make it pop.`
→ Blind slips into hype/exclaim (red). Knowledge base returns warm and on-voice (green).

---

## Capture list (film raw, any order)

1. **You to camera:** hook, the problem, the close (talking-head bits).
2. **Screen /agent:** click the first chip → **blind (left) goes red** (hype /
   exclamation spam), **knowledge (right) stays green** and warm. If a violation
   remains on the right, click **"Teach the agent from this mistake"** and watch
   a new rule drop into the Knowledge Base.
3. **Screen /thanks:** click **NVIDIA** → a warm, on-brand thank-you writes
   itself → point at "Record it in your voice on BlessOut."
4. **Screen /platform:** slow scroll past the church wedge + the Create / Fork /
   Rent / Earn cards.

## The 2-minute edit (beat sheet)

### 0:00-0:12 — HOOK  *(talking head, max energy)*
> "This is an AI agent that learned my brand's rules and won't break them. Watch."
*(cut to a 1-sec flash of the red → green fix.)*

### 0:12-0:28 — THE PROBLEM  *(talking head)*
> "Everyone's generating with AI now, and it drifts off-brand instantly. My own
> agent once designed a collab and mixed our logo with a partner's — a real
> branding no-no. The fix isn't a better prompt. It's a brand that *enforces
> itself* and *learns*."

### 0:28-1:05 — DEMO: IT ENFORCES + LEARNS  *(screen /agent)*
- Click the first chip (hype post). **[trim the wait]**
- Left: *"Blind — no knowledge — it screams in hype and exclamation points. Red.
  That is not our voice."*  Right: *"Same Nemotron model, reading the rules it's
  learned. Warm, confident, clean. Green."*
- Click **"Teach the agent from this mistake"** (if the right side ever slips). A
  rule animates into the Knowledge Base ("just learned").
> "It turns its own mistakes into rules and never repeats them. No retraining.
> The intelligence is in the knowledge base."

### 1:05-1:30 — DELIGHT: THANK A SPONSOR  *(screen /thanks)*
- Click **NVIDIA**. A warm, on-brand thank-you writes itself. **[trim the wait]**
> "Same brain, different superpower. It just wrote NVIDIA a thank-you in our
> voice — warm, human, not corporate — and I can record it in *my* voice and
> send it as a BlessOut e-card. On-brand deliverables with *soul*."

### 1:30-1:50 — THE BUSINESS  *(screen /platform)*
> "And this is a platform. Build a brand universe once — its canon, its rules —
> then fork it or rent it. Picture a beautiful comic universe that tells the
> truth of Christianity. What church wouldn't pay to rent that to make their own
> on-brand content? That's GitHub for brand universes."

### 1:50-2:05 — CLOSE  *(talking head, warm + confident)*
> "A brand that gets smarter and more soulful every time you use it. Powered by
> NVIDIA Nemotron. It's live right now. Come build the future with us."

Optional end card: the URL + "Powered by NVIDIA Nemotron."

## Energy + delivery
- Lead with the wow, explain second. Earn the next 10 seconds in the first 3.
- Say **"Nemotron"** out loud 2-3 times (sponsor-tech points reward the clear why).
- Warm and confident, not hype. No "you're already behind." (On brand.)
- Tight cuts. 15 min raw → 2 min of only the good stuff.

## Why Nemotron (the "why," for the bounty)
> "The brain is NVIDIA Nemotron, an open model built for agentic work, served on
> NIM. It does the generation *and* critiques its own output against the brand
> rules. Open infrastructure, not a hosted frontier API."
